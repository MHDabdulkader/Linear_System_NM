Introduction

Gaussian Elimination is a powerful method used in linear algebra to solve systems of linear equations. Whether you are a student delving into the world of mathematics or a professional dealing with complex mathematical models, understanding Gaussian Elimination is essential. In this tutorial, we'll explore the fundamental concepts of Gaussian Elimination and guide you through its step-by-step application.


What is Gaussian Elimination?

Gaussian Elimination is a systematic technique used to transform a system of linear equations into its row-echelon form, making it easier to find solutions. The method involves a series of elementary row operations performed on an augmented matrix representing the system.

Understanding Jacobi Iterative Method A Step-by-Step Tutorial


Introduction

Jacobian method or Jacobi method is one the iterative methods for approximating the solution of a system of n linear equations in n variables. The Jacobi iterative method is considered as an iterative algorithm which is used for determining the solutions for the system of linear equations in numerical linear algebra, which is diagonally dominant. In this method, an approximate value is filled in for each diagonal element. Until it converges, the process is iterated. This algorithm was first called the Jacobi transformation process of matrix diagonalization. Jacobi Method is also known as the simultaneous displacement method..

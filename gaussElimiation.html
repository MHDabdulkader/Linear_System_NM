Understanding Gaussian Elimination: A Step-by-Step Tutorial


Introduction

Gaussian Elimination is a powerful method used in linear algebra to solve systems of linear equations. Whether you are a student delving into the world of mathematics or a professional dealing with complex mathematical models, understanding Gaussian Elimination is essential. In this tutorial, we'll explore the fundamental concepts of Gaussian Elimination and guide you through its step-by-step application.


What is Gaussian Elimination?

Gaussian Elimination is a systematic technique used to transform a system of linear equations into its row-echelon form, making it easier to find solutions. The method involves a series of elementary row operations performed on an augmented matrix representing the system.
